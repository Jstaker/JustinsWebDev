Audience

  • The target audience of this site will be for anyone who wants to check what the weather will be like in the given areas that are provided on the website. Two examples are Bernard and Heidi.

  • Persona 1: Bernard Howard

  • Bernard plays lots of football. He enjoys playing in warm weather because he feels like he can perform better under nicer weather conditions. He will use the Cool Breeze site to see a forecast of what the weather will be like on gameday.

  • Persona 2: Heidi McKay

  • Heidi likes to travel on the weekends. She has a car that gets great mileage so she often drives to her travel locations. She will use the site to see what the weather will be like in the locations that she is traveling.
  • Scenario 1-A user would like to see a 10 day forecast
  • Scenario 2-A user wants to know the weather for the current time/day
  • Scenario 3-A user wants to know weather conditions for any given location
  • Scenario 4-A user wants to be able to easily navigate the website
  • Scenario 5-A user wants to feel like they will return to the site or refer it to a friend
