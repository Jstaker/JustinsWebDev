Audience

  • The target audience of this site will be for my classmates and anyone who lives in the areas where I display the weather.
  • Persona 1: A classmate
  • Persona 2: Someone who lives in the weather location
  • These two personas can see what the weather is like for the location that is being displayed/broadcasted, they can observe the map that is displayed in the upper section, find contact information for me, compare the weather that is being displayed to their current area, and enjoy the nice colors and design of the site.
